Yasserstain is a Serbian social media star, author, and singer, best known for his YouTube channel of the same name, which has amassed more than 600k subscribers. He created the channel on November 23, 2009, and uploaded his 1st video titled Yasserstain Trailer! very soon after. He began filming comedy sketches with friends from elementary and secondary school who were also YouTubers at the time, some of which were DruggedChicken, ViscTV, and GejmerSaKlise. As there was no monetization in place on YouTube at the time, more and more of his real-life friends appeared in guest or regular roles for fun. It wasn’t until 2015 that Yasserstain became an organized, more serious group of friends that would regularly appear on-screen, after which they upgraded the video quality and began filming 3-7 videos per month.

They also decided to release “Top 5” form of videos, and added weekly or biweekly Q&A video series under the name “ZBOG SIRA”. Yasserstain also began uploading vlogs, organizing fan meetups and quickly began creating and recording original music videos with the help of DexLik, associated with the Mix Music Company, a musical equipment store, often using suggestions of their viewers. On September 20, 2016, Yasserstain decided to revive an old channel that he created back in 2010, and began uploading Behind The Scenes (BTS), and bloopers, as well as videos that didn’t make it to the main channel. On January 15, 2019, Yasserstain also put into motion a new project, a podcast series named SIROVA PRIČA (RAW STORY), discussing various casual and serious topics. Yasserstain has also amassed a fanbase online with more than 250k followers on Instagram, more than 150k followers on Facebook, and more than 10k followers on Twitter.

As a Singer

Yasserstain has openly admitted that he’s not very comfortable singing himself, and lets other members of his team sing in music videos while he helps with production or writes lyrics. However, he was heard singing in some of those, such as NIKAD TEŽE BURAZ (2015), KALIMERO (2016), and CHARLIE JE POSTAO GAMER (2017).

On November 27, 2016, Yasserstain sang the intro song to the Naruto animated TV series, and on September 28, 2018, uploaded a video with his friend Irina “Inna” Arsenijević where he sang theme songs from well-known anime series such as Pokemon, Smash Mouth, DBS, DBZ, Shaman King, Kimi No Na Wa, and others.
