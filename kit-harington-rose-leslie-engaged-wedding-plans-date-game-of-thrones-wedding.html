GAME of Thrones stars Kit Harington and Rose Leslie announced their engagement in September last year.

And the acting duo the knot on a beautiful sunny Saturday in Scotland, with several of their GoT co-stars in attendance. Here's the latest.

When did Kit Harington and Rose Leslie get engaged?

The Sun exclusively revealed the pair got engaged in September 2017.

The couple met on the set of the hit show when they played onscreen lovers, but they are now getting married in real life.

A source said: “They’re yet to set a date, but told friends and family last week that they’ve got engaged.

“Kit’s known for ages he’s wanted to marry Rose but he wanted them to get a house and settle down a bit first.

“After moving in together in January he felt it was the right time to take the next step.”

The pair met on the set of GoT, where Rose played Ygritte from seasons two to four and Kit still plays her character’s former lover Jon Snow.

But they kept their love quiet for four years, officially stepping out together for the first time last year.

After we broke the news, the engagement was formally announced in The Times newspaper.

The couple's ad stated: "Mr K.C. Harington and Miss R.E. Leslie.

"The engagement is announced between Kit, younger son of David and Deborah Harington of Worcesterhire, and Rose, middle daughter of Sebastian and Candy Leslie of Aberdeenshire.

When did Kit Harington and Rose Leslie get married?

The Game of Thrones stars married on Saturday, June 23, 2018, in a sprawling castle in Aberdeenshire, Scotland.

Among the guests at Leslie's 12 Century family castle were Emilia Clarke and Peter Dinklage who both star in the HBO series.

Sophie Turner, who plays Kit's sister in Game of Thrones, also made her way to the remote Aberdeenshire location.

Marcus Mumford - lead singer of the Mumford and Sons was also at the event and was expected to perform at the reception.

Speaking on The Jonathan Ross Show in October 2017, Kit explained how he had got in touch with Game of Thrones producer to let them know filming schedules would have to change for their nuptials.

He said that as they met on the show, production is going to be shut down so everyone can attend the bash.

He explained: “I rang him up and I said, ‘I’m getting married and it’s your fault actually’... I think for the final season he is so stressed that he’s reached that peaceful level.

"I was like, ‘You need to factor in a Game of Thrones wedding by the way.’ They [the cast] have all got to be there so the whole thing has got to shut down."

Are Kit Harington and Rose Leslie related?

Technically Kit and Rose are related though the pair are yet to comment on the distant relation.

While fans are delighted the couple are to wed, one couldn't help but notice something a miss after reading both their Wikipedia pages.

Taking to Twitter they wrote alongside screenshots of both profiles: "Cool that Kit Harington and Rose Leslie are engaged but also they are related???"

Indeed, Kit's early life section reads: "Through his paternal grandmother, Lavender Cecilia Denny, Harington's eighth-great-grandfather was Charles II of England".

Meanwhile, Rose's says: "Her mother is Candida Mary Sibyl 'Candy' Leslie (nee Weld) of Clan Fraser, maternal great-granddaughter of Simon Fraser, 13th Lord Lovat, and descendant of Charles II of England".
