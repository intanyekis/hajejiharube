When sunglasses were first invented, they did not possess much variety. They had the sole purpose of protecting human eyes against the brightness of sunlight. However, now sunglasses are viewed as fashion accessories. Since its origination, the whale definition of a pair of sunglasses has changed into something entirely else.

Seeing their rising demand, eyewear brands come up with new and exquisite designs continually. Although the primary purpose of these shades is to protect the eyes against harmful UV rays and blue light, they are viewed as expensive accessories that symbolize wealth and power.

Louis Vuitton Cyclone Sunglasses

Louis Vuitton Malletier or Louis Vuitton is a French brand known for making the most fashionable and often the most expensive fashion accessories. LV’s Cyclone Sunglasses became massively popular when the Korean boyband BTS began advertising them.

These shades are a huge deal amongst BTS fans or the BTS Army. However, what makes the Louis Vuitton Cyclone Sunglasses a trend in 2021 is that it is a glamorous accessory that comes at $870.

Cartier Paris 18k Gold sunglasses

The famous Louis-François Cartier founded ‘Cartier Paris’ in 1847, and it remained a family business till 1964 until it was taken over by d new investors. Today it is one of the most prestigious fashion and jewelry brands worldwide.

The 18 karat Gold Sunglasses by Cartier Paris are also amongst the most expensive shades in the world. They come with a hefty price tag of about $25,000. The primary reason for its hype is that they come with a retro feel, and the design is similar to the fashion sense of the 1980s.

Lugano Diamonds Sunglasses

The high cost of the Lugano Diamond Sunglasses is, first, the diamond-adorned rim and frame and, secondly, the high brand value of Lugano Diamonds. It is a company based in California that describes itself as “One of the finest jewelers in the world.”

This pair has been designed by Lugano Diamonds in collaboration with the renowned designer Barton Perrera. It comes at an extravagant cost of about $27,000

Bentley Platinum Sunglasses

The British automaker, Bentley, has been a subsidiary brand of the Volkswagen Group since 1998. It specializes in creating luxury automobiles, and in celebrating its 100th anniversary, Bentley decided to develop unique luxury eyewear.

The result was the creation of the Bentley Platinum Sunglasses. At the price of $45,276, this unique and classy eyewear qualifies as one of the costliest pairs of sunglasses in the world.

Gold And Wood 253 Diamond glasses

The production house of the reputed brand Gold adWood is based in Luxemburg, and the founder is none other than Maurice Leonard. This company, established back in 1995, is a famous manufacturer of designer eyewear.

The Gold and Wood 253 Diamond glasses come at a jaw-dropping price of $55,000. This elegant rimless piece is adorned with 253 qualitative graded diamonds along with 22 princess-cut diamonds.

Bulgari Flora sunglasses

Bulgari is a luxury brand based in Italy that specializes in more than just sunglasses. It manufactures premium leather goods, watches, jewelry along with some exquisite pairs of sunglasses.

The Bulgari Flora Sunglasses have a white gold body studded with diamonds. Solid black frames surround the lens, and overall it has a beautiful design with a subtle touch of exoticism. It is undoubtedly one of the most expensive, with a cost of approximately $59,000.

Luxuriator Due Style 23

Luxuriator by Franco is a German brand that handcrafts all of its luxury accessories. The Due Style 23 is a handcrafted design with an unprecedented and unmatched look. The buyer also has the option of customizing the pair.

It comes with 18 karat gold covering the body, while the rims are made using buffalo horns to maximize comfort. This unique piece comes at a fancy price of about $65,000.

Emerald Sunglasses - Shiels Jewellers

Jack Shiels from Adelaide, Australia, founded the Shiels Jewellers luxury brand back in 1945. Today, the brand is owned by Albert Bensimon and is famous for creating some of the most exclusive designs.

When it comes to sunglasses, the Emerald by this Australian Jeweler is genuinely one-of-a-kind. The manufacturer sources the emerald used in the lenses for about five years, and it takes another three months to cut them. It is currently the third most expensive eyewear globally, with an astounding cost of $200,000.

DG2027B Dolce and Gabbana

Dolce and Gabbana, popularly known as D&G, is an Italian luxury brand established by designers Domenico Dolce and Stefano Gabbana back in 1985 in Legnano. Presently, it is one of the most sought-after fashion labels in celebrity circles.

The DG2027B eyeshades by D&G is reportedly the second most expensive pair of sunglasses in the world, with a mindblowing price tag of $383,609. Yet again, D&G aced the perfect eyewear design by combining the Mediterranean style with a modern look for the DG2027B.

Chopard De Rigo Vision Sunglasses

The Chopard eyewear items are a collaboration of Swiss expertise with an Italian design. The Chopard Sun 2020 Collection designed by De Rigo Visions got special attention from the masses.

This pair ranks as the most expensive sunglasses pair globally in 2021 with an unbelievable price of $408,000. These designer eye shades are made using 24 karat gold and weigh over 60 grams. The glasses are crafted in such a manner that they can emit light of their own.

The basic features that sunglasses usually serve are protecting the eyes, but what makes them expensive is the kind of material used in their making, like gold, diamonds, or sapphire. The other times, it is simply the high rising value of a luxury brand.
