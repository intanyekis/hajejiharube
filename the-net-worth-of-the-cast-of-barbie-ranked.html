Barbie has been a cultural icon for over 60 years, serving as a global toy phenomenon with over a billion dolls sold. Directed by acclaimed filmmaker Greta Gerwig, the first-ever live-action Barbie movie boasts a stellar cast. Margot Robbie stars as the title Barbie character, while Ryan Gosling plays Ken. The supporting cast includes comedians, musicians, and award-winning actors. With its satirical take on society's expectations, Barbie hopes to inspire young girls while entertaining audiences worldwide.

The highly anticipated Barbie film is set to hit theaters in 2023 alongside Chris Nolan’s Oppenheimer. With its all-star cast, buzzworthy director, and cultural significance as the first Barbie movie, it's generating major hype in Hollywood and beyond.

America Ferrera - $16 Million

Ugly Betty star America Ferrera joined the Barbie cast in the role of Gloria. She first found fame on the hit ABC comedy series, winning an Emmy Award and Golden Globe Award. Ferrera has since starred in films like How to Train Your Dragon and Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants. Her activism for women's rights and immigration reform has further boosted her profile. Through her varied movie and television career Ferrera has accumulated a net worth of $16 million.

Simu Liu - $19 Million

Chinese-Canadian actor Simu Liu skyrocketed to fame as the Marvel superhero Shang-Chi. Now he has leaped into the world of Barbie as a Ken. Liu had humble beginnings, working multiple jobs before his acting career took off. After starring in the sitcom Kim's Convenience, he landed the coveted role of Shang-Chi in 2019. The 2021 film, Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings was a box office smash grossing over $430 Million worldwide and cementing Liu as Marvel's newest star. According to Wealthy Gorilla, Liu currently boasts an impressive $19 Million net worth.

Michael Cera - $20 Million

Known for playing awkward teens, Michael Cera joined the cast of Barbie, donning the role of Allan. He first found fame on cult TV shows like Arrested Development and films like Superbad and Juno. Cera often collaborates with directors like Judd Apatow, poking fun at his quiet, nerdy persona. While he's stayed somewhat under the radar recently, Barbie marks a high-profile return for the actor. Cera's current net worth stands at a respectable $20 million.

Dua Lipa - $40 Million

Global pop superstar Dua Lipa makes her acting debut in Barbie, playing the role of Mermaid Barbie. At just 27 years old, the British/Albanian singer has taken the music world by storm. Her 2020 album Future Nostalgia spawned major hits like Don't Start Now and Levitating. She's won 3 Grammy Awards and sold over 75 million records worldwide, making her one of the fastest-growing music sensations in the world. Lipa now boasts an impressive net worth of $35 million owing to her chart-busting music and multiple tours.

Margot Robbie - $40 Million

Playing the lead role of Barbie, Margot Robbie has already established herself as a bonafide A-lister. She is the recipient of multiple accolades, including Oscar nominations for I, Tonya, and Bombshell. Apart from being a terrific actress, Robbie has modeled for Calvin Klein, appeared on Time's Most Influential list, and runs her production company, LuckyChap Entertainment. Being one of the most sought-after actresses in the world, Robbie drew a staggering $12.5 Million paycheck for her titular role in Barbie, boosting her net worth to an estimated $40 Million, according to Cosmopolitan.

Rhea Perlman - $60 Million

Comedy veteran Rhea Perlman joined the cast of Barbie, donning the role of the creator of Barbie, Ruth Handler. She's best known as the feisty waitress Carla on the classic sitcom Cheers and won four Emmy Awards during the show's 11-season run. Perlman’s other notable works include movies like Sunset Park, Matilda, 10 Items or Less, The Sessions, and I'll See You in My Dreams. Her decades-long acting career has earned Perlman an impressive $60 million net worth.

Ryan Gosling - $70 Million

Global heartthrob Ryan Gosling stars opposite Margot Robbie in Barbie as the lead Ken. Since his breakout role came in 2004's The Notebook, Gosling has shown off his versatility in films like La La Land, Drive, The Big Short, and Blade Runner 2049. Being one of Hollywood's highest-paid leading men, Gosling charged a whopping $12.5 Million for his role in Barbie, taking up his net worth to around $70 million according to Celebrity Net Worth.

John Cena - $80 Million

John Cena, a WWE icon with a net worth of $80 million, takes on the role of Kenmaid in 2023’s Barbie. Cena, who has been a prominent figure in wrestling for nearly two decades, is also known for his roles in comedy films such as Trainwreck and Blockers. His voice acting credits include the Transformers spin-off, Bumblebee, and the animated series Dallas & Robo. As Cena transitions from wrestling to Hollywood, his role in Barbie is expected to introduce him to a different demographic.

Helen Mirren - $100 Million

Helen Mirren, a distinguished British actress, joined the Barbie cast, taking on the role of the narrator. Mirren, who has won an Oscar, a Tony, and four Emmy Awards throughout her illustrious career, is renowned for her portrayals of three iconic queens: Elizabeth I, Elizabeth II, and Catherine the Great. She has starred in action films such as Excalibur, Red, and the Fast & Furious series. Mirren's regal yet playful on-screen persona has endeared her to audiences worldwide and made her one of the most celebrated actresses to ever grace the industry. In a career spanning over five decades, Mirren has garnered a massive net worth of $100 Million, according to National Today.

Will Ferrell - $160 Million

Will Ferrell, a comedy superstar with a net worth of $160 million, completes the Barbie cast by playing the role of CEO of Mattel. Ferrell, known for his roles in SNL and Anchorman, has consistently delivered big laughs in popular films like Elf, Talladega Nights, and Step Brothers. His production company, Gary Sanchez Productions, has produced successful projects like The Virgin Gary and HBO's Succession. Ferrell has previously taken the mantle of the highest-paid actor in Hollywood, drawing staggering paychecks of $20 Million each for movies like Land of the Lost, Semi-Pro, Talladega Nights: The Ballad of Ricky Bobby, and Bewitched, to name a few.

With its star-studded cast, Barbie is already sparking significant buzz in Hollywood. From award-winning actors to musicians and comedians, the film boasts immense talent and range. It will be exciting to see this diverse group bring the iconic Barbie franchise to life in a modern, empowering way. Under Greta Gerwig's visionary direction, this could begin a new era for the legendary Barbie brand.
