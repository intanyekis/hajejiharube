Kylie Jenner, Tyga Hold Hands at Kanye West's 'Famous' Video

 Posted on June 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  252 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Back on? Kylie Jenner took ex-boyfriend Tyga as her date to the premiere of Kanye West‘s controversial “Famous” video in L.A. on Friday, June 24 — just hours after the “Rack City” rapper sparked rumors that they were back on.

The pair, who split in May, were spotted holding hands as they left the premiere of the NSFW video at The Forum in Los Angeles.

The show of togetherness comes on the heels of the 18-year-old reality star’s appearance in new boyfriend PartyNextDoor‘s music video for “Come and See Me,” which was released on Thursday, June 23.

Earlier on Friday, Tyga, 26, posted and then deleted an Instagram photo of Jenner sitting on a bed along with the caption, “They always come back …” She was pictured wearing a skintight nude ensemble from the day before and the lipstick queen also tagged Tyga in a pic of the outfit that she posted on Instagram on Friday.

Tyga and Jenner joined her mom, Kris Jenner, sisters Kim, Khloé and Kourtney Kardashian, and Kourtney’s ex Scott Disick at the “Famous” premiere.

As Us Weekly previously reported, the video features a number of celebrities — including Rihanna, Taylor Swift, Donald Trump, Caitlyn Jenner and Amber Rose — nude and lying on a bed on either side of a naked Kimye.
