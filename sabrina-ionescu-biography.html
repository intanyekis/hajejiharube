Who is Sabrina Ionescu?

Sabrina Ionescu is an American basketball player.

The public noticed her playing style during her college career with the Oregon Ducks.

She is currently playing with a team in New York Liberty.

Age, Parents, Siblings, and Education

She was born on 06 Dec 1997 in Walnut Creek, California, United States.

The name of her mother is Liliana Blaj and her father’s name is Dan Ionescu.

Sabrina’s ancestry is Romanian-Caucasian and has a twin brother, Edward.

Her elder brother is Andrei Ionescu.

Talking about her academics, she attended Miramonte High School from 2012 to 2016.

Sabrina Ionescu then joined the City College of San Francisco but later transferred to the University of Oregon.

Sabrina Ionescu- Professional Life, Career, Awards

Sabrina Ionescu started to play basketball during her high school and University days.

She then in 2020 was selected by an American basketball team named New York Liberty.

Sabrina Ionescu plays as a guard and for her playing style, she has won several titles.

Sabrina was three times Nancy Lieberman Award (2018, 2019, 2020), two times John R. Wooden Award (2019, 2020), and two times Wade Trophy (2019, 2020).
