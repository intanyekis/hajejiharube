Regarding Sally - The Young and the Restless

 Posted on June 13, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1105 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Sally is just depressing at this point, this girl can never win, all the way from childhood to now. For those who don’t know her backstory, her parents wanted nothing to do with her and sister when they were kids so they dropped her off with her Grandma and never looked back, and then in 2017 she comes to LA to try to revive Spectra Fashions (Her great aunt Sally’s fashion house) which started off as a super fun storyline, it was nice to see B&B going back to its roots, only to have the character Bill Spencer take it all away from her and commit arson on the building because he wanted the building for himself (which never even ended up happening), while working at Spectra fashions she dated Thomas Forrester, they were great together, had amazing chemistry only for them to be broken up leaving Sally devastated because Thomas got back with his ex Caroline. Then she started a romance with Wyatt, which was also great, amazing chemistry, very fun couple only for them to once again destroy that pairing too by throwing Sally under the bus and making her a villain against Wyatt’s ex-girlfriend by kidnapping her and holding her hostage and of course Wyatt ended up reuniting Sally’s victim the ex-girlfriend in the end leaving Sally out in the cold again. Then she comes to Genoa city for a fresh start and gets involved with Jack/Summer/Kyle, got a job at jabot only for her to once again be written as a villain plotting with Tara to make Summer go away, which resulted in her being fired from Jabot and Jack ending his relationship with Sally and never interacting with her again after that and being shunned by Lauren/Summer/Kyle. Then she finds a new circle for another fresh start, (Adam, Nick, Chelsea, Chloe) which of course starts off with Chelsea bullying and bashing Sally at any chance she gets with Tried pushing herself on Adam with Adam acting like he wanted nothing to do with her at first, Sally finally gets her wish and Adam begins a romance with Sally which was great, only for her to cheat on him with his brother and later get pregnant needing a paternity test, Adam ends up being the father, they come up with a great, meaningful name for the baby honoring Adam’s mother only for the baby to die. She can never catch a break, it’s sad at this point seeing a character as depressing as her. She has so much potential, Adam can help her re-open Spectra Fashions in Genoa City so she can do what she originally wanted to do, which is to revive Spectra Fashions and continue her great aunt Sally’s legacy.
