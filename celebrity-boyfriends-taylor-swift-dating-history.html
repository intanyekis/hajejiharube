How Many Celebrity Guys Has Taylor Swift Dated?

 Posted on July 22, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1266 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Taylor Swift is one of the greatest artists of her generation. She has broken numerous records and ranks in the Guinness Book Of World Records. However, Swift's dating history is a record itself. Indeed, Swift's dating history puts Leonardo DiCaprio and other so-called players to shame. Not only does Swift break music records, but she also broke a lot of guy's hearts and then wrote songs about it.

Taylor Swift is one of the most dominant musicians. She adores her fans and is one of the most inspirational artists today. Swift has released chart-topping singles, and her albums sell exceedingly high numbers. She is one of the most influential and recognizable celebs in the industry.

Of course, Swift has an awe-inspiring dating history as well. She has dated a long list of handsome and famous men. Swift isn't looking to get tied down but is always willing to give love a chance. There is a risk in dating Swift. She is known for writing and singing about several failed relationships. It's safe to assume, if you date Taylor Swift, she' going to break your heart and write a number one hit single about it. It's time to take a closer look at Swift's dating history. How Many Celebrity Guys Has Taylor Swift Dated?

13 Joe Jonas

Taylor Swift's first public relationship was with Joe Jonas. They dated from July 2008 to October 2008 and were the "it" couple during that time. At the time, Swift was on the verge of becoming a major star. Of course, it was also Swift's first public breakup. Jonas broke up with Swift in a now-infamous 27-second phone call. Swift wrote two songs about the breakup, "Last Kiss" and "Forever And Always." A few years later, she released "Holy Ground," which brought her full circle.

12 Lucas Till

Taylor Swift is always open and honest with her fans. Well, she's also honest with her boyfriends too. In 2009, Taylor Swift started dating Lucas Till for a few months. Indeed, they met on the set of Swift's music video for "You Belong With Me." They were the perfect couple, but that was the problem. Till and Swift felt they make better friends than a couple. They mutually broke up and went their separate ways. Of course, Swift went on to be in several high-profile relationships.

11 Taylor Lautner 

From August 2009 to December 2009, Taylor Swift dated Taylor Lautner in a highly publicized relationship. They met on the set of Valentine's Day in which they play love interests. However, by the time the movie hit theatres in 2010, they were no longer dating. Indeed, Swift's song "Back To December" references her dumping someone in December. Most fans assume the song is about Swift breaking up with Lautner.

10 John Mayer

At the age of 19, Taylor Swift dated musician John Mayer. Of course, Mayer has an impressive dating history himself. They dated from December 2009 to February 2010. It might have been a short relationship, but it had long term damage. A heartbroken Swift then released the not so subtle single "Dear John." The brutal and personal song was clearly about Mayer. Of course, Mayer responded with "Paper Doll," but Swift left a lasting impression.

9 Cory Monteith

In 2010, rumors swirled that Taylor Swift dated Corey Monteith from April to May in a brief relationship. Indeed, they attended various events together and took several pictures. However, neither Monteith nor Swift ever confirmed the stories. Rumors further suggest that Swift's song "Mine" is about their brief relationship. In 2013, Swift was devastated upon hearing the news that Monteith suddenly passed away.

8 Jake Gyllenhaal

From October 2010 to January 2011, Taylor Swift dated Jake Gyllenhaal in a relationship that had everyone talking due to the ten year age gap. Eventually, Gyllenhaal ended the relationship. Of course, Swift turned to her music for comfort. The single "Last Kiss" makes references to the relationship, as does the song "All Too Well." The song details the scarf incident in which Swift's scarf ended up in Gyllenhaal's possession. Indeed, he wore the scarf on several occasions since the breakup.

7 Eddie Redmayne 

Rumors suggest that Taylor Swift and Eddie Redmayne dated from October 2011 to January 2012. They met when Swift auditioned for a role in Les MisÃ©rables. During that time, Swift and Redmayne often hung out together in New York, which sparked rumors they were dating. Regardless, Redmayne and Swift deny that they dated for three months at the end of 2011.

6 Zac Efron

For years, Taylor Swift and Zac Efron deny that they ever dated. Of course, nobody cares what they say as the rumors persist. Rumors suggest Swift and Efron dated from February 2012 to March 2012. The speculation started when they promoted the movie The Lorax. They even wrote a parody song about the relationship and performed it on The Ellen DeGeneres Show. Swift and Efron hope the rumor is a thing of the past.

5 Connor Kenndey 

In 2012, Taylor Swift and Connor Kennedy had a memorable Summer romance between July and October. Kennedy is the grandson of Robert F. Kennedy. However, dating a member of the famous Kennedy family isn't what made headlines. Indeed, Swift was 22-years-old, and Kennedy was 18 at the time. They had people talking, but the relationship soon came to an end. Later, Swift released the song "Begin Again," which possibly makes references to her relationship with Kennedy.

4 Harry Styles

Taylor Swift and Harry Styles seemed like a dream couple, but it soon turned into a nightmare. Swift and Styles dated for a brief time in 2012. On the surface, they appeared to be the perfect couple as they went for walks in Central Park and kissed on New Year's Eve. Of course, they also had some bad moments like the time they were in a snowmobile accident. Swift's song "I Knew You Were Trouble" is about her relationship with Styles, and the single "Out Of The Woods" also references the relationship.

3 Calvin Harris

Taylor Swift and Calvin Harris dated from February 2015 to June 2016. Indeed, it seemed that Swift had finally settled down. Swift and Harris were a very public couple and often shared images on Instagram. Swift and Harris went on vacation and celebrated significant holidays together. Rumors suggest Swift's hit single "We Are Never Ever Getting Back Together" is about the bitter relationship. However, Swift fans are split on the single as others believe the song is about Jake Gyllenhaal.

2 Tom Hiddleston

Taylor Swift wasted no time in moving on after breaking up with Calvin Harris. Indeed, just days after Swift began dating Tom Hiddleston. The couple was together from June 2016 to September 2016. They were instantly a high-profile couple and even earned the couple name Hiddleswift. Of course, the relationship soon came to an end. The music video for "Look What You Made Me Do" features dancers wearing I Love T.S. t-shirts, which Hiddleston once wore when they were dating.

1 Joe Alwyn

Taylor Swift is currently in a relationship with Joe Alwyn. They first started dating in 2017, and things have been going great. Swift and Alwyn keep their relationship out of the spotlight. They recently started opening up and sharing pics on Instagram. Rumors suggest the couple is happy together, and Swift might have finally found the one. Indeed, Swift's fans believe that she wrote many songs about Alwyn, including "Ready For It" and "Gorgeous."
