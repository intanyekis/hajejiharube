Beauty runs in the family of Gigi Hadid and her younger sister, Bella Hadid. But this isn’t all. Hard work and perseverance are two other traits that the sisters are best known by.

Gigi, 26, is the eldest daughter of Mohamed and Yolanda Hadid. Two years later, the former couple welcomed their second child – a girl, Bella, who is now 24 years old.

The supermodels also have a younger brother named Anwar, and he also works as a model just like their mom.

Yolanda is, perhaps, the most statuesque star in The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. She was a gentle and loving mom to her children, who sometimes made cameos on the reality TV show.

But it didn’t take long before Gigi and Bella made a name for themselves that goes beyond their surnames. Here’s a look at the sisters’ net worth, as well as some of the things that they have achieved at their young age.

Gigi Hadid – Net Worth $29 Million

Gigi’s net worth has increased exponentially over the years. In 2018, Forbes estimated her net worth at $9.5 million. Three years later, Celebrity Net Worth revealed that the supermodel is now worth a whopping $29 million.

The 26-year-old is one of the highest-paid models of her generation, thanks to her contracts with Maybelline, Evian, Versace, BMW, Tommy Hilfiger.

But Gigi’s modeling career started after she was discovered by Paul Marciano of Guess when she was still a baby. She modeled for Baby Guess but didn’t pursue an actual modeling career until 2011. A year later, she once again teamed up with Marciano for a Guess campaign.

In 2013, Gigi signed with IMG Models, and not too long after she already graced her first New York Fashion Week. She has also appeared on the cover of several magazines like CR Fashion Book, Vogue, Teen Vogue, Joy Magazine, Harper’s Bazaar, and more.

In 2014, Gigi starred in Tom Ford’s eyewear’s autumn and winter campaign, as well as Tom Ford’s Velvet Orchard Fragrance, Tom Ford Beauty.

Today, Gigi is already married to Zayn Malik. And they have a daughter together.

Last month, her dad boasted about her success and revealed that Gigi is self-made.

On his Instagram account, Mohamed uploaded a photo of Gigi and his granddaughter with 20 things that the public might not know about the model.

First on the list is that Gigi never asked money from her parents even though they are both well-off.

“What the 20 things that you did not know about Gigi Hadid? 1. Self-Made… never took a single Dollar from her parents,” Mohamed wrote.

Gigi and Malik currently live in Manhattan that she purchased for $4 million. The property boasts of a huge bathroom and receiving area. The property also features eccentric colors that suit the model and her husband’s taste.

During last year’s lockdown, Gigi and her husband temporarily moved to her family’s ranch in Pennsylvania to quarantine there.

Bella Hadid – Net Worth $25 Million

Bella has also an impressive resume to her name. At 24 years old, her net worth is already estimated at $25 million.

Even though this figure is slightly smaller than her older sister’s net worth, Bella doesn’t have anything to worry about because she’s also one of the highest-paid models of her generation.

The sisters’ careers have been very successful that their combined net worth is already estimated at $54 million.

To date, Bella has already partnered with designers and brands like Victoria’s Secret, Calvin Klein, Diane Von Furstenberg, Savage X, Moschino, Alexander Wang, Versace, and more.

Despite her Lyme Disease diagnosis, Bella still pursued her modeling career and is pretty good at it. She also signed with IMG Models in 2014 when she was just 16 years old.

Bella’s first modeling gig was in Flynn Skye’s commercial project. Shortly after, she also graced the New York Fashion Week when she modeled for Desigual.

A year after her runway debut, Bella had the opportunity to appear on the cover of Seventeen magazine. She also graced the covers of Teen Vogue and Evening Standard.

Due to her success, Bella reportedly gets paid approximately $27,000 for one sponsored post on Instagram. No wonder, her $6 million net worth in 2018 has already quadrupled today.

Bella currently resides in New York, where she bought a penthouse condominium for $6.1 million.
