Education

Perry was only 7 years old when her mother enrolled her into the Ensemble Theater Young Performers Program in Houston, where she was taught singing, dance, acting, and stage production.

She graduated from Kashmere High School in 2006, where she learned to dance and play the piano. The same year, DomiNque graduated with a degree in Theater/radio, television & film from Prairie View A&M University.
