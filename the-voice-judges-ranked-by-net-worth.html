'The Voice' Judges Ranked By Net Worth

 Posted on May 21, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1009 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Judges on The Voice are almost as important as the contestants who are trying to win the competition! The judges get to pair up with musicians who they think are the most talented in order to help cultivate successful music careers.

RELATED: The Voice: Here's What The Judges Think Of The Show

Some of the incredible judges from The Voice who did not have net worth high enough to land on this list include Nick Jonas with a net worth of $50 million, Kelly Clarkson with a net worth of $45 million, Jennifer Hudson with a net worth of $25 million, and CeeLo Green with a net worth of $22 million. The rest of the judges have net worths that landed them in our top 10 list.

10 John Legend: $75 Million

John Legend has a net worth of $75 million and a huge reason why is because his music career has been going on for such a long time and he has been awarded 11 Grammys! Who can forget the song he wrote for his wife Chrissy Teigen called “All of Me“? The lyrics are so romantic and meaningful… That was one of the most popular songs to be played in weddings for several years after its release in 2013.

9 Blake Shelton: $80 Million

Blake Shelton has a net worth of $80 million and has been a judge on The Voice since the very first season. Allegedly, he receives $13 million per season which has definitely added to the overall load of his net worth! Another source of his income comes from his country music career. He has been nominated for nine Grammys over the year and every single one of his albums has landed in the top 10 Billboard Hot 100 Chart.

8 Adam Levine: $120 Million

Adam Levine has a net worth of $120 million and stayed on the show seasons 1 through 16. After season 16, Adam Levine decided to part ways with the show but he was a huge staple up until that point. He has released a lot of amazing songs over the years and has even taken home three Grammys. Honestly, a lot of his friends believe he should have taken home Waymore Grammys and that since he has been nominated 10 times. His Grammy awards come from his time being the lead singer of Maroon 5.

7 Gwen Stefani: $150 Million

Another beloved judge from The Voice is Gwen Stefani who has a net worth of $150 million. Before she was a solo artist, Gwen Stefani was part of a group called no doubt and during that time, she went to Grammy awards. Gwen Stefani has an incredible voice and is known for her bleach blonde hair and bright red lipstick. Somehow, she is 51 years old but she looks not a day over 25.

6 Alicia Keys: $150 Million

With a net worth of $150 million, Alicia Keys was a familiar face on The Voice for three seasons. Out of 29 Grammy award nominations, she has taken home 15 and has truly made a name for herself in the world of R&B music.

RELATED: Alicia Keys’ Huge Salary On ‘The Voice’ Isn’t The Only Way She’s Amassed Her $100 Million Fortune

Something else that is very interesting about Alicia Keys is the fact that she probably doesn’t wear a full face of makeup anymore and she still looks drop-dead gorgeous.

5 Christina Aguilera: $160 Million

Christina Aguilera has a net worth of $160 million from her time as a musician and her time starring on The Voice. She was part of the starting lineup of judges back in 2011. She has taken home five Grammy awards over the years and just like other talented musicians on this list, her fans definitely feel like she should’ve taken home more Grammy awards by now since she has such a powerful voice to match her beautiful face and personality.

4 Miley Cyrus: $160 Million

Miley Cyrus has a net worth of $160 million that she started gathering at a very young age when she starred on Disney channel's Hannah Montana. She started working as a judge on The Voice during season 11 when she was only 23 years old making her one of the youngest judges on the panel, ever. Miley Cyrus’s music is really fun and energetic! She has collaborated with big names in the industry like Britney Spears and French Montana.

3 Usher: $180 Million

Usher falls in third place for having the highest net worth of judges from The Voice with $180 million. He has been considered one of the best R&B singers since the mid-2000s and has taken home eight Grammys over the years.

RELATED: Does Usher’s Visit On ‘The Voice’ Hint To A Longer Return?

One of the biggest things Usher could’ve ever done in his career was discovering Justin Bieber! He discovered Justin Bieber through YouTube videos and fully put Justin Bieber on the map. Justin Bieber fans are eternally and forever grateful to Usher.

2 Pharrell Williams: $200 Million

Pharrell Williams falls in second place with a net worth of $200 million! Although he has released several songs as a musician himself, he also does a lot of work behind the scenes as a producer and that is where a huge bulk of his financial wealth and success has come from. Something else that is very interesting about Pharrell Williams is that he seemingly never appears to age. It looks like he has the same youthful glow that he had 15 years ago.

1 Shakira: $300 Million

Our first-place winner for the judge from The Voice with the highest net worth is Shakira. She has a net worth of $300 million! The beautiful Latin singer is considered an international icon which is why she has amassed such wealth over the years. She has taken home three Grammy awards but more impressively, she’s taken home 12 Latin Grammy awards! She performed at the Super Bowl halftime show in 2020 with Jennifer Lopez and it was a hit.
