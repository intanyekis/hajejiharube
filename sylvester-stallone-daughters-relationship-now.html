An Adorable Story About Sylvester Stallone’s Relationship With His Daughters

In the year 2021, two of Sylvester Stallone’s daughters, Sistine and Sophia, launched a podcast called Unwaxed. As a result of that podcast, Sylvester’s fans now have the opportunity to learn about his daughters. After all, Sylvester’s other fascinating daughter Scarlet has appeared on the podcast as well.

When people listen to the Unwaxed podcast, it won’t take long for something obvious to become clear, Sylvester’s daughters have lives that aren’t solely defined by who their father is. On the other hand, the Stallone daughters also aren’t shy about talking about their relationships with their dad.

During one episode of the Unwaxed podcast that was especially telling about Sylvester Stallone’s relationship with his daughters, Sophia Stallone told a revealing story. According to Sophia’s story, Sylvester gives his daughters dating advice that reveals how much he wants them to value themselves.

"I was telling my mom about how I have a date this week, and my dad suddenly comes out of nowhere. He was dropping me some golden nuggets. He was like, ‘can I butt in here?’”

“He was saying about being patient for the right one and that the way that the guy treats you early on really is an indicator of how his overall personality will be in the relationship."

"He said it might be next week, or it might be when you're 35 years old, but choose happiness and true compatibility than chasing the clock."

After Sophia Stallone revealed the advice her father Sylvester Stallone gave her, Sistine Stallone chimed in to praise their father’s perspective on dating. "Sometimes he is Shakespearian when it comes to dating."

In all reality, the advice that Sylvester Stallone gave his daughter Sophia Stallone was far from groundbreaking. Despite that, his remarks reveal how much Sly adores all of his daughters and wants to make sure they don’t rush into anything.

It also has to be said that the fact that Slyvester popped up out of nowhere with his pearls of wisdom is adorable.

Other Facts About Sylvester Stallone’s Relationship With His Daughters

On top of Sophia Stallone’s aforementioned story about what kind of father Sylvester Stallone is, the action star’s daughters have revealed several other anecdotes about their dad. Some of those stories unsurprisingly reveal that Sylvester Stallone is always there for his daughters.

In the past, Sylvester Stallone’s daughters have revealed that their grandfather was a hairdresser. Since Sylvester learned a few tricks from his own father, it turns out that he would dye his daughter’s hair when they were growing up.

Even though Sylvester Stallone has given his daughters great dating advice, it turns out that he can also be a big pain when it comes to their love lives. In fact, Stallone is so protective of his daughters that he can be so intimidating to their potential love interests that he has been nicknamed the “Grim Reaper”.

On the other hand, Sylvester Stallone’s daughter have been open that when things go wrong in their love lives, it is their father they turn to. The reason for that is that, as Sistine Stallone revealed, is that Sylvester’s daughters would sometimes get him to break up with their boyfriends for them over text.

“He goes, ‘Let me tell you how a guy thinks’, and so he’ll write it to a text for us (sic): ‘Breaking up with a guy’. I think most of the time our boyfriends don’t know Sly is breaking up with them! It’s very quick, simple.”
