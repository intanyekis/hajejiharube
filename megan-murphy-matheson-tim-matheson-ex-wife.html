Megan is not the first wife of the American actor. Before Tim Matheson got married to Megan, he was previously married to Canadian actress, Jennifer Leak. The duo was together for 2 years after getting married in September 1968. They got divorced in 1971.

According to the actor, his marriage to Jennifer Leak was an immature decision, which was why it did not last. They separated 6 months after they got married and finalized their divorce a few years later.

Tim waited for about 14 years before getting married to Megan. After their divorce, he got together with Elizabeth Marighetto, whom he married in 2018. The couple lives in Hollywood, California.
